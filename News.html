Chamber of Commerce News

Meetings of The Chamber

Our regular monthly meetings are the first Thursday of each month at 6:00pm in the Redruth Town Council Offices. Occasionally we will schedule a social meeting to just chat and relax.

As the largest Chamber in Cornwall our District serves many communities. Working together we can make a difference for All.

  • Redruth
  • Illogan
  • Lannar
  • Pool
  • Portreath, Scorrier, Stithians
  • St Day and Carharrack, Carn Brea

As the representative body for our Business Community, the Chamber works in partnership and cooperation with other organizations.

  • Redruth Town Council.
  • Cornwall Chamber of Commerce
  • Cornwall Council
  • Local CICs.
  • Schools.
  • Civic Organizations

Membership

Membership is open to all businesses within the District. Our Constitution is available for download.

Membership Check list

  • Completed Membership Application.
  • First Years Subscription
  • Attend a meeting of The Chamber
  • Provide a brief summary of your business.
  • Include contact info and business hours.

    • This info is requested so we may place your business on our digital media and marketing platforms.
