Schedule of Events

Meetings of The Chamber

Our regular monthly meetings will alternate between the first Wednesday and Thursday of each month with the next scheduled meeting on Wednesday April 6, 2016 at 6:00pm in the Redruth Town Council Offices. Our Guest Speaker for the April Meeting is Mel Colton-Dyer from The Cornwall Chamber of Commerce. The May meeting will be on Thursday May 5th 2016 in The Council Chambers at 6:00pm.

On 2 August 2016 the Man Engine will make it's way through Redruth. This is part of the "Tinth" celebration of Cornish Mining Heritage. It will come into town, stop at the old Brewery Site and continue down to the intersection at the bottom of West End where it will stand up to it's full 30meter height. We are hoping to support this as a Chamber.
